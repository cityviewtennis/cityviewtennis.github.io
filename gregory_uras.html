Teaching hightlights

  • Gregory has been teaching tennis for 22 years
  • All ages and levels, including high-performance players at the national and international levels
  • His greatest thrill in tennis is watching players develop their game and helping to foster their love of the sport

Teaching history

  • 2020–Present: City View Tennis Club
  • 2014–2020: Noble Tennis School at the Ottawa Athletic Club
  • 2009–2014: Badminton Racquet Club and Sir Winston Churchill Club in Toronto
  • 2003–2005: Teaching at a local tennis club in France (while competing at the same time)

Playing history

  • Trained and competed in France as a Junior
  • 5-time Provincials’ Junior Singles Champion
  • 2-time member of France’s Top 8 Juniors, alongside Richard Gasquet and Jo-Wilfried Tsonga
  • Competed in European Championship and International Tennis Federation (ITF) tournaments

Fun facts

  • Winning record 1–0 vs Gaël Monfils (U12)
  • Teamed up with Tomáš Berdych in European Open (Doubles) (U12)
  • Has taught players from 2½ to 88 years of age
